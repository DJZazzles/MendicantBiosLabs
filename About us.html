About Us

Mendicant BIOS Labs is a Progressive Experimental Electronic music label originating in the United States.
The Labs consists of musical veterans Z. Salieri and Gomi (formerly A.I.Xibiades)
who have both previously released music independently, as well as under the label Abstract Machine Music.
The rising talent Zazzles now joins their ranks.
The musicians formed the label in January of 2023, unified by the strong inspiration of Japanese underground EDM and Artcore,
with the intent of not only sharing their experimental creations, but also opening the door for a new audience to uncharted artistic avenues.
